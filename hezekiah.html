Hezekiah Gibril

Director of Product

Hezekiah Gibril serves as the Director of Product at SmartEdge Electronics, responsible for the curation and management of the product catalogue. He works closely with suppliers and brands to ensure that SmartEdge stocks the most relevant, high-quality electronics for the Kenyan market, from flagship smartphones to everyday accessories.

Hezekiah has a deep interest in consumer electronics and keeps a close eye on global trends to inform SmartEdge's product strategy. His eye for quality and value has been central to building a catalogue that customers trust. He holds a background in procurement and product management.
