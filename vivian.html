Vivian Akinyi

Director of Marketing

Vivian Akinyi is the Director of Marketing at SmartEdge Electronics, responsible for brand strategy, digital marketing, and customer engagement. She joined the founding team with a clear vision of how SmartEdge should present itself to Kenyan consumers — as a trustworthy, modern, and accessible electronics brand.

With a background in communications and digital media, Vivian has led campaigns that have grown SmartEdge's online presence significantly since launch. She is deeply interested in how technology intersects with everyday life in Kenya and uses that insight to inform the company's messaging and outreach.
