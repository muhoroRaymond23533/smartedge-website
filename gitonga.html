Gitonga Paul

Director of Logistics

Gitonga Paul is the Director of Logistics at SmartEdge Electronics, overseeing the end-to-end delivery process that gets products from the warehouse to the customer's door. He manages relationships with courier partners and works to ensure that delivery timelines are met consistently across Nairobi and the wider Kenya.

With a background in logistics and distribution, Gitonga brings practical expertise to one of the most critical parts of any e-commerce operation. He is focused on building a delivery network that is fast, affordable, and dependable — key pillars of the SmartEdge promise to its customers.
