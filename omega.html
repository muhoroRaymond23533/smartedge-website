Omega Chepkirui

Director of Customer Experience

Omega Chepkirui is the Director of Customer Experience at SmartEdge Electronics, responsible for ensuring that every interaction a customer has with the brand — from browsing to post-purchase support — is smooth, satisfying, and trustworthy. She leads the customer service team and sets the standards for how SmartEdge handles enquiries, returns, and feedback.

Omega has a strong background in customer relations and retail, having worked across several consumer brands before joining SmartEdge. She believes that great customer experience is the foundation of any successful e-commerce business and works tirelessly to make that a reality at SmartEdge.
